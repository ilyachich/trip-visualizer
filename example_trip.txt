Japan Trip - 7 Days (Tokyo, Kyoto & Osaka)

Day 1 – Arrival in Tokyo
We land at Narita International Airport in the morning and take the Narita Express to the city.
Check in to the Park Hotel Tokyo in Shimbashi.
In the afternoon, stroll through Shinjuku and visit Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden.
Dinner at Ichiran Ramen in Shinjuku – the solo ramen booth experience is a must.

Day 2 – Tokyo Sights
Start the day at Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa – arrive early to beat the crowds.
Walk along Nakamise Shopping Street.
Head to Akihabara Electric Town for electronics and anime goods.
Late afternoon: Shibuya Crossing for the famous scramble intersection.
Dinner at Gonpachi Nishi-Azabu (the "Kill Bill" restaurant) in Minami-Aoyama.

Day 3 – Tokyo: Harajuku & Teamlab
Morning: Meiji Jingu Shrine then Harajuku Takeshita Street for quirky street fashion.
Teamlab Borderless digital art museum in Odaiba – book tickets in advance!
Dinner: Sushi Saito in Minato – pre-booking required, one of Tokyo's best.

Day 4 – Shinkansen to Kyoto
Morning: check out of Park Hotel Tokyo and take the Shinkansen (bullet train) from Tokyo Station.
Arrive Kyoto Station and check in to Hyatt Regency Kyoto in Higashiyama.
Afternoon: wander the Higashiyama District and Sannenzaka/Ninenzaka stone-paved lanes.
Visit Kiyomizudera Temple at sunset.
Dinner: Nishiki Market food stalls then kaiseki dinner at Nakamura in Nishiki.

Day 5 – Kyoto Deep Dive
Morning: Fushimi Inari Taisha shrine – hike the torii gate path at dawn.
Mid-morning: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and Tenryu-ji Temple gardens.
Afternoon: Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and Ryoan-ji Zen garden.
Dinner: Pontocho Alley – pick any restaurant along this atmospheric narrow lane.
We chose Pontocho Kappo Masuda for traditional Kyoto cuisine.

Day 6 – Day Trip to Nara, then Osaka
Morning: take a local train from Kyoto to Nara.
Visit Todai-ji Temple (giant Buddha) and feed the free-roaming deer in Nara Park.
Return to Kyoto, pick up bags from Hyatt, and continue by Shinkansen to Osaka.
Check in to Conrad Osaka in Nakanoshima.
Evening stroll through Dotonbori: see the Glico Man sign, eat takoyaki at Wanaka and try kushikatsu at Daruma.

Day 7 – Osaka & Departure
Morning: Osaka Castle and its surrounding park.
Explore Kuromon Ichiba Market (Osaka's Kitchen) for fresh seafood and street food.
Lunch: okonomiyaki at Mizuno in Namba.
Head to Kansai International Airport for the evening flight home.
